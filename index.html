Our History

With over 25 years in the Bridal Industry in Japan, Voice Pro Japan has developed a deep pool of talented and professional voice actors and actress. Our English and Japanese bilingual staff helps to facilitate and support overseas clients to find a most suitable voice talent for every project. We are motivated by our rich talent and expertise to bring Japanese and other voices to the world.
